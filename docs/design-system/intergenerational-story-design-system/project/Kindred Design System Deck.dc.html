SCREEN 01
The conversation
The core loop. A family member's question sits at the top in serif; the elder simply speaks. Everything below is what was said.
VOICE-FIRST
The 96px mic is the only loud element. Photo and keyboard are equal, quieter siblings.
TRANSCRIPT
Speech becomes serif body text in place — already reading like a memoir.
PHOTOS IN CONTEXT
A photo can be attached to the exact moment it's mentioned, not a separate album.
SCREEN 02
The finished story
The conversation, edited gently into a memoir page. Photographs lead; the original audio is always one tap away.
EDITORIAL
Newsreader at 22px with a drop cap. It should feel printed, keepsake-worthy.
PROVENANCE
People, place and year are tappable tags — the quiet hooks for genealogy data later.
