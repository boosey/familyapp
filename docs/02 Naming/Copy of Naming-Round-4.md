# Naming — Round 4: Saga- · Ark- · Stitch/Patchwork Variations

*Built from your liked set: Arkive, Sagahold, Sagakeep, Stitchkin, Patchwork, patchwork.family, Ark, plus Sagastry, Legasee, SagaArk, Sagakin, SagaStitch, SagaQuilt. Below: each vein expanded with many variations, conflict notes inline.*

**Quick availability read on your picks:**
- **Arkive** — *crowded.* Several live companies (a pet app, a beauty firm, a culture DAO, a retailer). None in family-memory, but it's a popular coinage — ownable only with a clear modifier/class.
- **Patchwork** — *crowded.* A YC startup, a co-parenting app ("Patchwork Family"), a gifting platform. Hard to own bare; a compound is safer. "patchwork.family" itself didn't show as taken.
- **Sagahold, Sagakeep, Legasee, Stitchkin, Sagastry, Sagakin, SagaArk** — appear clear in this space. The coined Saga- words are your most ownable territory.

- **Sagakeep** ✓ — saga + keep (castle keep + to preserve). Spine + warmth.
- **Sagavault** — saga + vault. Most secure/permanent feel.

**Saga + lineage/kin:**

- **Sagaline** — saga + line of descent.
- **Sagafolk** — saga + folk. Warm, plain.
- **Sagaroot** — saga + roots.

**Saga + craft/weave (bridges to your quilt seed):**
- **Sagastry** ✓ — saga + tapestry. Telling + weaving. My favorite coined Saga word.

- **Sagaquilt / SagaQuilt** ✓ — saga + quilt. Most literal craft blend.
- **Sagaloom** — saga + loom. Telling + the inherited weaving frame.
- **Sagaweave** — saga + weave.

**Saga + vessel (bridges to your trunk/ark seed):**
- **SagaArk** ✓ — saga + ark (the vessel carrying what survives). Two strong images; reads big.
- **Sagachest / Sagacoffer** — saga + the trunk.
- **Sagatrove** — saga + trove (treasure). (Note: "Trove" is busy elsewhere, but blended it's distinct.)

**Saga stretched into a coined single word (most trademark-clean):**

- **Sagely / Sagewise** — leans on the "sage/wisdom" pun.
- **Sagaria** — saga + -ia (a land/place of sagas). Distinctive, world-like.

*Ark = the vessel that carries what must survive a long passage. Great gravity. "Arkive" is crowded, so these lean on other ark-blends.*

- **Ark** ✓ — bare, weighty, mythic. Ownable only with a modifier (`ark.family`, `theark`).
- **Arkive** ✓ — ark + archive. Crowded but on-meaning; would need a clear family-class modifier.
- **Arkin** — ark + kin. Soft, ownable, hides "ark" + "kin." Nice.
- **Arkward** — ark + -ward (toward/keeper). (Risk: reads "awkward" — probably kill it.)
- **Arkhold / Arkkeep** — ark + hold/keep. Parallel to Sagahold/Sagakeep.
- **Arkana / Arcana** — the vessel of secret/precious knowledge; mystical, distinctive. (Arcana has game uses — check.)
- **Arkloom** — ark + loom. Vessel + inheritance-weave.
- **Arkfolk / Arkkin** — vessel + family.
- **Everark** — ever + ark; "always carrying." Permanence + vessel.
- **Heirark** — heir + ark; the inherited vessel. Distinctive blend of two of your seeds.

## VEIN C — STITCH / PATCHWORK / QUILT (craft, making-whole-from-scraps)

*This vein literally is your synthesis engine: many scraps → one cloth. "Patchwork" is crowded bare, so compounds and stitch-blends are safer.*

**Stitch-:**
- **Stitchkin** ✓ — stitch + kin. Warm, crafted, ownable. Strong.
- **Stitchwell** — stitch + well. Gentle, "stitched well / a deep source."
- **Stitchward** — stitch + -ward (keeper of the stitching).
- **Stitchhold / Stitchkeep** — stitch + hold/keep.

- **Sagastitch** ✓ — (also Vein A) telling + stitching.

**Patchwork- (compound to make it ownable):**

- **Patchkin** — patch + kin. Short, warm.

**Quilt- / coverlet:**

- **Quiltline** — quilt + lineage.
