## 1. What the name has to carry (constraints)

From the vision doc, unchanged across every round:

- **The attic, not StoryWorth.** The competitor is the unlabeled shoebox / trunk / the one relative who "knew all the family history." The product *is* the well-kept version of the attic.
- **A living institution, not a one-time gift.** It "outlives any single contributor." Not a disposable app-store-cute name.
- **Dignity and warmth for elders.** No condescension, no baby-talk, no slang. A grandmother should say it with pride.
- **Perpetual chronicle.** One chronicle underneath, many modes on top; "render a snapshot from a stream that never stops."

## 2. Hard rulings (do not re-litigate)

- **`clan-` is BANNED.** Spoken aloud, "Clan-X" is phonetically indistinguishable from **"Klan-X."** For a heritage/bloodline product this is the worst possible adjacency. This kills Clanark, Clankeep, Clanhearth, Clanharbor, Clancoffer, Clantrove, Clancapsule and every other clan- combo **regardless of clean availability.** (Decided 2026-07-16. Clanark had been the session frontrunner before this ruling.)
- **`kin-` is the single most picked-over root.** Every legible kin-compound comes back with the `.com` gone: Kinkeeper (live eldercare product), Kinstory (`.com`+`.co` held), Kinsaga (two live family apps, one branded "Family Operating System"), Kinchest (`.com` parked). The more on-thesis the kin-word, the more likely it's already a product.
- **Burned words (multiple live products each) — avoid bare use:** Heirloom, Airloom, Kindred, Keepsake, Trove, Locket, Saga, Griot, Hearth (solo).
- **The legibility ↔ ownability vise.** The names that read instantly (kinchest, sagakin, legasee, loomwell, everlore) are *taken*; the ones that are *available* need a root you have to teach (kith-). This is the central tension — pick which side you'll accept.
- **Active land-rush.** Many collisions were registered in **2026** (kithloom Jun, kithlore May, loomkeep Jun, hearthark Feb, kithkeep Jan…). This territory is being claimed monthly; a clean name today may not be clean in a month.
