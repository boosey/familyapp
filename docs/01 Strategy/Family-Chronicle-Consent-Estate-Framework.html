Consent & Estate Framework

The institutional layer (Mode 5): who controls the archive, who sees what, what happens to a person's stories after death, and who holds the keys across generations.

⛔ The sequencing rule: build this framework before any avatar feature ships. You cannot ethically build the powerful feature until the governance that constrains it exists.

Five foundational principles

Principle 1

Their words only

Avatars retrieve real recordings — never generate new statements in someone's voice. The brightest line.

Principle 2

Explicit & revocable consent

Never assumed from silence. Captured, logged, scoped, and (for the living) reversible.

Principle 3

Living ≠ deceased

The living consent in real time. The dead only per pre-death consent. Silence is a "no."

Principle 4

Family veto, grief protected

The family can decline or retire any feature. Off-ramps and human support; not therapy.

Principle 5

AI always disclosed

Synthesized prose, restored photos, and avatars are visibly labeled. No one is misled.

The avatar consent gate · every check must pass

1

Consent in the ledger

Pre-death consent (deceased) or active real-time consent (living). No consent = no avatar.

▼
2

"Their words only" enforced

Retrieval over real recordings. If they never recorded an answer, the avatar says so — it doesn't invent one.

▼
3

Mutual consent + family governance

Both the person consented to be represented and the family governs who may interact.

▼
4

Disclosure + a retirement procedure

Visibly labeled as AI; a dignified way to pause or take it down if it interferes with healthy grief.

Living vs. deceased · the ethical heart

Present & in control

The Living

  • Co-author and consent in real time
  • Set & change their own privacy tiers
  • Edit by voice; revoke anytime
  • Consent is a living, renewable conversation
✓ Continuously reversible
Represented only as they permitted

The Deceased

  • No pre-death consent = no posthumous avatar
  • Mutual consent required for any interaction
  • Family veto + built-in retirement procedure
  • Enforced by the steward via the ledger
⛔ Silence is not permission
