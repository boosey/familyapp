Release Roadmap

Wedge-first, risk-retiring. Ship the sharpest wedge fastest to validate the core loop, then layer enrichment, engagement, and ethics-gated features.

⏱ Ordered by dependency & risk, not calendar. Built with AI coding agents, so the unit isn't weeks — it's "what must be true before the next phase is buildable." A phase ends on exit criteria, not a date.

The phase ladder · each phase makes the next buildable or safe

PHASE0

The Spine

One data spine everything reads from — entry model, identity graph, minimal consent ledger & permissions.

Build: entry model + identity graph (core IP)  ·  Buy: auth, storage, DB

Retires: an un-retrofittable data model
▼
PHASE1

The Wedge — Elder Voice Capture (MVP)

An elder talks with zero friction; family receives a real story in their actual voice. Plus the asked-question loop.

Build: interviewer behavior (oral-history craft), zero-friction elder entry  ·  Buy: transcription, telephony, LLM

Retires: do elders engage? are the stories good enough to care?
▼
PHASE2

Make It Land — Payoff Surface & Light Engagement

Explore surface (timeline, feed, voice player) + the social loop and weekly digest. Retain, don't just capture.

Build: explore surfaces, trigger cadence rules  ·  Buy: nothing major new

Retires: capture decaying into the digital shoebox
▼
PHASE3

The Moat — External-Data Enrichment

Fuse narration with records. Start free/high-coverage (FamilySearch, Chronicling America, Ellis Island, census). DNA out of scope.

Integrate: data providers via API / user-linked accounts  ·  Build: the matching & enrichment layer

Retires: "just another AI memoir tool"
▼
PHASE4

The Institution — Stewardship & Governance

Steward console, consent ledger, custody hand-off, story will, time-gated release, full AI disclosure.

Build: bespoke — trust depends on legibility  ·  Buy: durable backup; answer "what if the company disappears?"

Retires: growth outrunning governance · unlocks the avatar gate
⛔ ETHICS GATE — avatars cannot ship until Phase 4 governance exists
PHASE5

The Dangerous Feature — Avatars (gated)

Voice avatars first, living-person before posthumous. Every one passes the 4-check consent gate. "Their words only" — never a generative ghost.

Build: consent enforcement, retrieval constraint, retirement procedures  ·  Buy: voice synthesis / rendering

⚠ Introduces the product's highest risk — which is why it's last
▼
PHASE6+

The Widening — the rest of the north star

Real-time capture (Mode 2), ambient (Mode 6), live co-piloted calls, mysteries & geolocation, DNA (opt-in, late), legacy & forward-time features, heritage language.

None on the critical path — all valuable later, on a stable, trusted base.

⏳ Competitive timing: the AI voice-memoir space is filling fast. Land the wedge (Phase 1) in real families early — even thin — before the window narrows. The compounding moat comes in Phases 3–4.

Through-line: start with what needs only the family & the calendar → add what needs data → then what needs time. Never let a powerful feature precede its guardrail.
